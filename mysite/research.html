Research interest

I am interested in the biochemical and molecular study of peptides from amphibians and other organisms. In particular, I am interested in antimicrobial and pharmacological active peptides. Through scientific research I would like to understand the biological function of these molecules from an evolutionary point of view.
Antimicrobial resistance is a world wide problem. The abuse of antimicrobials and incomplete treatment has lead to the increase of antimicrobial bacteria. In hospital settings the risk of been infected by a resistant pathogen microorganism is even higher. Leaving us sometimes without any treatment available. I am interested in document the antibiotic resistance in the hospital setting and also to analyse the resistance genes asociated with horizontal transmission.
Amphibian diseases such as chytridiomycosis and ranavirus are associated with amphibian population declines worldwide. I am interested in the diagnosis of these diseases by molecular biology.

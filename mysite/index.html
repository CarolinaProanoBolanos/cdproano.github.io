Research interest

I am interested in the biochemical and molecular study of peptides from amphibians and other organisms. In particular, I am interested in antimicrobial and pharmacological active peptides. Through scientific research I would like to understand the biological function of these molecules from an evolutionary point of view.

Projects

  • Conservation of Ecuadorian Amphibian Diversity and Sustainable Use of its Genetic Resources
  • Antimicrobial resistance
  • Amphibian declinations related to epidemic diseases such as chytridiomycosis

I am an Ecuadorian biologist interested in Microbiology and Biochemistry. I became interested in the studying the chemical hidden in the frog when my professor of Zoology introduce me epibatidine -an alkaloid identified in the skin of the Ecuadorian poison frog Epipedobates tricolor Epibatidine acts as a pain killer, but it acts under nicotinic receptors instead of opiod receptos. Therefore not being addictive!. An analog of epiatidine named ABT-494 is in development phase II for the treatment of patients with reumatoidea arthritis. My facebook
